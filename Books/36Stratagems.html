The Thirty-Six Stratagems by Sun Tzu

The thirty-six stratagems has, no surprise, thirty-six parts.
These can be broken down into 6 sections.
These 6 sections can be further broken down into 2 sections.

  1. Stratagems For When You Are Winning
    1. Stratagems For Advantageous Situations
      1. Sneak Across The Ocean In Broad Daylight
    2. Stratagems For Opertunistic Situations
      1. Create Something From Nothing
    3. Stratagems For Attacking Situations
      1. Stomp the Grass to Scare the Snake
  2. Stratagems For When You Are Losing
    1. Stratagems For Confused Situations
      1. Remove the Firewood from Under the Pot
    2. Stratagems For Deception Situations
      1. Replace the Beams with Rotten Timbers
    3. Stratagems For Desperate Situations
      1. The Beauty Trap
